Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This policy explains the information Skinica processes while the application is in development and how that information supports the service.

Information Skinica processes

  • Account information such as email address and profile name.
  • Profile choices including skin type, cosmetic concerns, and goals.
  • Face photos submitted for a requested cosmetic analysis.
  • Analysis results, routine steps, shelf products, wishlists, and progress records.
  • Basic technical records needed for security and reliability.

Face photo handling

A submitted face photo is encoded and sent to the analysis service only when the user requests a scan. The current application records the scan as “ephemeral” and does not upload the original photo to Skinica’s permanent scan-image storage. The analysis provider may process the image transiently to return results. Face photos and Amazon content are not used to train Skinica models.

Service providers

Skinica currently uses Supabase for authentication, database, and Edge Function infrastructure. Google Gemini may process user-requested analysis or assistant content. Authorized retail APIs may provide product images, current prices, availability, and external links. These providers process data under their own applicable terms.

Purpose and legal basis

Data is processed to provide requested features, secure accounts, maintain user preferences, improve reliability, and meet legal obligations. Where consent is required, users may withdraw that consent, although some features may then stop working.

Retention

Account and analysis records are retained while needed to provide the service or until a valid deletion request is completed, subject to legal and security obligations. Licensed Amazon content is cached only for the permitted period; current implementation limits general commerce cache to 24 hours and price freshness to one hour.

Sharing

Skinica does not sell face photos or personal profile data. Data may be shared with infrastructure and analysis providers only as needed to operate requested features, protect the service, or comply with law.

User choices

Users may request access, correction, export, or deletion of their personal information, subject to applicable law. Use the contact page and do not attach sensitive images or credentials to a public request.

Children

Skinica is not directed to children under the minimum age required to consent to data processing in their location.

International processing

Service providers may process information in countries other than the user’s own. Appropriate contractual and technical protections will be used where required.

Security

Skinica uses access controls, server-side secrets, row-level database security, and encrypted network connections. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Skinica approaches commercial launch. The date above will change when material revisions are published.

Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the Skinica contact page. Legal operator and dedicated privacy contact details will be completed before commercial launch.
