About

A calmer way to understand a skincare routine

Skinica is a mobile application in development for cosmetic skin analysis, routine planning, product organization, and educational skincare guidance.

What Skinica does

A user can capture a face photo, review visible cosmetic focus areas, receive a morning and evening routine, and organize products in a personal shelf or wishlist. Product suggestions are grouped around the user’s latest analysis and stated goals.

How product recommendations work

Skinica determines product fit from its own analysis and product metadata. Retail partners do not determine fit scores. When authorized retailer content is available, Skinica may display current pricing, licensed images, and an external purchase link.

What Skinica is not

Skinica is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease. Users with persistent irritation, pain, sudden changes, or other health concerns should speak with a qualified healthcare professional.

Current status

Skinica is currently in development and testing. Features, supported regions, and retail integrations may change before commercial launch.
