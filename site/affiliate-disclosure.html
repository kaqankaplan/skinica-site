Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Skinica may use affiliate links to support development and operation of the service.

Amazon disclosure

As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.

What this means

If a user follows an eligible link from Skinica and completes a qualifying purchase, Skinica may receive a commission. The user is not charged an additional fee because of that commission.

Recommendation independence

Affiliate commission does not determine Skinica’s analysis results, concern matching, or fit scores. Product suitability is calculated independently using Skinica’s own cosmetic analysis and product metadata.

Retailer information

Retailers control product images, current prices, availability, checkout, shipping, returns, and customer service. These details can change after Skinica displays them. Skinica limits retailer content caching and hides stale price information.

External checkout

Retail purchases are completed in the retailer’s application or external website, not inside Skinica. Skinica does not receive or process retail payment card details.

Questions

Questions about affiliate relationships can be submitted through the contact page.
